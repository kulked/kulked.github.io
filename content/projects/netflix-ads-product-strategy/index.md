## Overview

A comprehensive product strategy for Netflix's **ads-supported subscription tier** —
designed to revive subscriber growth, open a new revenue stream, and strengthen
Netflix's flywheel of content investment.

---

## Why an Ads-Supported Tier?

| Driver | Rationale |
|---|---|
| 🚀 **User Growth** | Revive subscriber base by offering a lower-cost entry point |
| 💰 **Better Monetisation** | Monetise price-sensitive users who would otherwise churn |
| 🔄 **Flywheel** | Unique content → user attention → ad revenue → more content |
| 🛡️ **Fight Competition** | Counter ad-supported offerings from Disney+, Hulu, HBO Max, Peacock |

---

## Value Proposition

> **A premium TV platform to reach GenX, Millennials, and GenZ —
> over 70% of whom spend the most time consuming video on streaming services.**

**Key Differentiators:**
- Favorable demographic mix: 33% Millennials · 26% GenX · 22% Baby Boomers · 19% GenZ
- Even gender split (51% female / 49% male)
- 70% of Netflix subscribers binge-watch — firm grasp on user attention
- 9 of the 10 most Googled TV shows in 2022 were on Netflix
- ~10% of total TV viewing share — #1 streaming service

---

## Product Goals

| Stakeholder | Goal |
|---|---|
| **Subscribers** | Engage users with delightful ad experiences without degrading the viewing experience |
| **Advertisers** | Connect brands with relevant audiences to accomplish marketing outcomes at high ROI |
| **Netflix** | Revive user growth; monetise user attention |

---

## Key Ideas (GROW Framework)

| Pillar | Description |
|---|---|
| **CREATE** | Easily create new objective-oriented campaigns |
| **GROW** | Expand advertiser reach to new, relevant target audiences |
| **CONTEXTUALIZE** | Contextual ads to maximise consumer engagement |
| **OPTIMIZE** | Optimise delivery and supply to safeguard consumer experience |
| **MEASURE** | Critical interaction measurement for actionable campaign insights |
| **REPORT** | Transparent campaign reporting with accountability on ad spend |

---

## Advertiser Segments

**Priority now:** Retail · Entertainment & Media · FMCG · Auto · Travel & Leisure

**Deprioritised for later:** Financial Services · Pharma (due to regulatory scrutiny)

**Use Cases Addressed:**

| Funnel Stage | Objective | KPI |
|---|---|---|
| Awareness | Connect with prospects at scale | Reach · Impressions · Brand Lift · CPM |
| Consideration | Drive interest in products | Qualified Leads · CPVC · CPL |
| Conversion | Drive on/offline purchases | Orders · Revenue · ROAS |
| Loyalty | Drive cross/re-purchases | Orders · Revenue · LTV · ROAS |

---

## Netflix Ads v1 — Solution Snapshot

| Area | v1 Details |
|---|---|
| **Ad Formats** | Slate (incl. QR code) 5s · Video non-skippable 15s / 30s |
| **Placements** | TV app — Pre-roll · Mid-roll |
| **Pricing & Ops** | CPM · Managed service |
| **Audiences & Targeting** | Content affinity · Demo · Device · Genre · Show |
| **Measurement** | 3P Viewability · Brand lift surveys · Reach / Impressions / Frequency |
| **Ad Serving** | 3P ads manager (DV360 · The Trade Desk) + client-side insertion |
| **Ad Load** | 6 mins / hour |

---

## High-Level Roadmap

| Phase | Year | Features | Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| **MVP** | Yr 1 | A/B test on 5% US traffic · No targeting/freq cap · Slate + Pre-roll + Mid-roll · CPM + QR codes | 25M MAU · $1.5B |
| **Scale** | Yr 2 | In-house ads manager + decisioning · Custom audiences · Content affinity · Self-serve reporting | 35M MAU · $2.5B |
| **Optimise** | Yr 3+ | ML-powered ranking · Cohort-based frequency capping · Programmatic Guaranteed · Pause ads · Sponsorship | 45M MAU · $3.5B |

---

## Success Metrics

- **Revenue:** Ad revenue growth from $1.5B → $3.5B over 3 years
- **Subscribers:** 25M → 45M MAUs on the ads tier
- **Advertiser ROI:** Brand lift survey scores · ROAS benchmarks vs. comparable streaming platforms
- **User Experience:** <X% subscriber churn attributable to ad experience
