## Executive Summary

- Genie Rewards system launch in **2 months**
- **Objective:** Drive acquisition, conversion, and retention across the Ajaib ecosystem
- Cross-functional execution required to ensure smooth rollout
- **Success defined by:** Low migration risk · High adoption · Measurable ROI within 90 days

---

## Migration Goals & Success Metrics

### Primary Goals

| Goal | Detail |
|---|---|
| 🟢 **Zero disruption** | Smooth migration with no user-visible reward issues |
| 🚀 **Day-1 campaigns** | Referral + onboarding campaigns live from day one |
| 📈 **5% uplift** | In funded account activations within 90 days |
| 💰 **3–5x ROI** | On rewards spend in the first 12 months |

### Migration KPIs

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Legacy reward data migrated | ≥99% with 0 critical errors |
| Reward pipeline uptime (month 1) | ≥99.9% |
| Unresolved user reward issues within 48hr | <1% |
| Reward transaction fraud rate at launch | <0.5% |
| DAUs engaging with new platform by week 2 | ≥70% |
| NPS/user feedback on rewards experience | Positive (survey week 2) |

---

## 8-Week Project Timeline

| Phase | Week | Key Activities |
|---|---|---|
| **Finalization** | 0–2 | Communicate legacy reward freeze window · Legal review of T&Cs · Finance: finalise budget, caps, accounting |
| **Migration Testing** | 3–4 | Data migration dry run · QA across all product verticals (stocks, QRIS, mutual funds) · Ops playbook creation |
| **Internal Pilot** | 5–6 | Sandbox campaigns with employees · Validate event triggers, disbursal accuracy, fraud monitoring · Prepare marketing creative + user comms |
| **Soft Launch** | 7–8 | Freeze legacy reward transactions · Move pending balances to new platform · Rollout to 10% of funded users |
| **Post-Launch** | Month 3+ | Full 100% rollout · Monitor stability, fraud, caps · Collect feedback · Expand campaign portfolio |

---

## Execution Plans by Function

### 📦 Product
- Own product-level UAT, business readiness, and success KPIs
- Appoint a migration owner per product to map legacy rewards to the new system

### ⚙️ Engineering
- Validate all integrations (event bus consumption/production, reward API calls)
- Execute staging-to-prod migration rehearsal and cutover script
- Maintain APIs for disbursal and real-time eligibility checks

### 🖥️ Platform / Infrastructure
- Monitor system health, scalability, and SLA adherence
- Drive cross-team alignment for integration and migration

---

## Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Fraudulent signups / referrals | High cost leakage | Strong KYC, device/IP checks, hard referral caps |
| Migration error / data mismatch | User dissatisfaction | Staged rollout, test migration, rollback plan, UAT checklist |
| Rewards T&C gaps or non-compliant cross-product rewards | Legal/financial penalties | Early legal reviews, audit logs, pre-approved comms |
| Budget overrun | Financial strain | Finance-controlled reward caps |
| User confusion | Low adoption | Phased launch, user education, accessible support |

---

## Next Steps & Ownership

1. Weekly cross-team standups — Product · Engineering · Growth · Marketing · Ops · Finance · Legal
2. Finalise pilot campaigns by **Week 3** — Marketing & Growth
3. Dry-run migration signoff by **Week 4** — Engineering & Product
4. Soft launch go/no-go decision — Product, Engineering, and Business Leadership
